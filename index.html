Mana Tithe

A tool to find the cheapest version of a Magic the Gathering deck list.

Paste in your deck list and Mana Tithe will look up each printing of the card and find the print with the lowest TCGPlayer Market Price, the price a card has recently been selling at. It does not guarantee you'll find a card for exactly that price. 



        

          

            
          

        


        

          This tool is intended to assist in budget brewing. The price calculation does not account for the cost of shipping from various storefronts, so the actual cost of the deck if purchased all at once will likely be substantially higher.
